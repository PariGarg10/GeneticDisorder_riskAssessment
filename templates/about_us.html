About GeneScan

GeneScan is a preventive health platform focused on helping families understand potential genetic and cardiovascular risk factors using data-driven insights. Our goal is to make early risk awareness simple, visual, and actionable for everyday users.

Our Mission

Make personalized risk screening accessible, clear, and useful for early preventive action across all age groups.

Our Method

Combine clinical inputs, family history, and machine learning to estimate individualized risk levels with explainability.

Our Promise

Deliver transparent guidance that supports informed conversations with healthcare professionals.
